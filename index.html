A little bit more about me

Born and raised in a small city in France, I've left this place for studies, promising myself I will discover as much as the world has to offer! While working in different cities in the country, I have spent a lot of time travelling on my spare time: mostly in Europe at first, but also in North Africa and more recently in America (mostly the south and sunny part -Caribbeans and Argentina, but also Canada). I currently live in the very north of France, in the great city of Lille, before aiming to leave the country once and for all! Next stop? Still a question mark.

After years spent in front of computers (beginning with an old Amstrad PC2086 in mid-90's), digging in the Internet' mysteries, people could have bet on computer sciences as a carreer choice. I first choosed to join a business school, aiming to do 'marketing stuff' at Coca-Cola, or even better, L'Oréal. Seems pretty conventional now that I think about it. Anyhow, I finally found my way to something a little bit more "computer-related". Or at least, digitally focused.

I started my work-life as a project manager in a small web agency. After more than 5 years working for customers, I then decided to make the big move: I joined a startup! I am now the proud product owner at Frizbiz, a platform which helps butterfingers meet doers who could make their housing the perfect home sweet home. My next move is pretty clear on my mind: sharping my coding skills to become a front-end developer.

Enough for the small talks!

Travels I have been lucky to enjoy

Argentina - Spring 2016

From Salta to the very North of the country, I have spent a week on the roads of Argentina during spring of 2016. This trip was actually a bit special, as I was part of the "Trophee Roses des Andes", a women-only roadtrip accross the Andes cordillera in Argentina.

Dominican Republic - Autumn 2015

I am lucky enough to have been able to discover the amazing Isla Ispañola the way few people do! As my in-law is from Dominican Republic, they really made their point not to go only sight-seeing in the touristic venues. I have spent twenty days discovering the mysteries of this country, from beaches to countryside, including manantials.

Greece - Autumn 2010 / Winter 2011

Greece is one of the countries I love the most of Earth. After several one-month trip there while I was a child, I had the opportunity to live in Athens for almost 6 months during my studies. From the tiny streets of Plaka to the crowded place of Syntagma, I have discovered the secrets of this antique city. I also made my way to some of the iconic Greek Islands.

A few other trips

Rome (Italy) - Autumn 2017

I have spent my 27th birthday in one of the most romantic city of the world: Rome! Discovering the little places of this antic city was exquisite. And food...oh food!

Morocco - Summer 2008

From the very North of Morocco to Ouarzazate (and its 57°C), I enjoyed a roadtrip far from the touristic venues, meeting moroccans and the amazing landscapes of the desert.

Lisboa (Portugal) - Autumn 2016

Attending the Web Summit, I had a little time to discover Lisboa's delicacies. From Time Out Market to the impressive Castel, everything made me want to go back.
